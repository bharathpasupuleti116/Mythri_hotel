Superior Rooms

Our Superior Room comprises of 1 Double bed or 2 Twin Beds, 2 Bedside Tables, a Desk & Chair. This room is larger than the Standard Room and is therefore spacious enough for an additional bed for triple occupancy. (Triple Room). The room is furnished with wall to wall carpeting, trendy furnishings and a balcony.

Deluxe Rooms

Our Deluxe room is a spacious single occupancy adobe. It has a single mega bed, a writing desk, and a chair. The rooms are specially designed to create the perfect setting for a romantic weekend. The rooms also provide you with bathrobes, mineral water & snacks upon arrival.
